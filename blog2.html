be loyal to your priorities!, November 12, 2023

111223

i made this post to remind everyone who are very goal-focused, such as myself, to not distract themselves and keep the grind going!

ofc, ok raman jud magcrush2 usahay. pero ang dili okay kay imo ipuli imo priorities sa imo crush! example, kung imo goal kay ma With High Honors karon na S.Y., focus rajud ana. dili kay makakita kag gwapo nya mukalit rakag chat niya ug "heyy" (speaking from experience). remember, ur future is on the line! stay focused, disciplined, and in control.

naa jud nay times mastress ta or kapuyon natas tanan. okay rajud kaayo mulet go kadali ug enjoy sa sa kinabuhi. pero, ayaw jud kalimti imohang goals, work until its finished! kung mana ug break, go back to work immediately, and i promise u worth it rajud sya in the end.

though sometimes, even though we worked our hardest, di nato makuha ato ganahan. if this ever happens, dont think about the results, but think about how you worked so hard in the process. makaingon jud ka na "i did my best." besides, this can also serve as a motivation for your future goals! life is always like that, it doesn't always give us what we want. but all we can do is keep going and going and going, until we make OURSELVES proud.

i know this was long but i really wanted to remind not only to the targeted people, but also myself haha! thank u for coming to my tedtalk
